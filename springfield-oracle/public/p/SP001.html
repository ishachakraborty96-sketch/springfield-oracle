The Full Story

In 'Two Cars in Every Garage and Three Eyes on Every Fish', Bart catches Blinky — a three-eyed mutant fish living downstream from the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant — and the story becomes a political scandal for Mr. Burns. In 2011, fishermen near the Embalse nuclear power plant in Córdoba, Argentina, caught a real three-eyed wolf fish, sparking international headlines. Nuclear contamination producing mutant wildlife went from Simpsons punchline to documented biological event.
